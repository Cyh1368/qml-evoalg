center_crx_entangler
</NAME>

<DESCRIPTION>
Introduce additional parameterized two-qubit entangling gates (CRX) on center-adjacent edges to enhance expressivity. Specifically add CRX gates on edges (1,8), (3,8), (5,8), and (7,8) after the existing CZ layer, each with its own per-block parameter. This increases the number of parameters per block (from 3 single-qubit params to 7 per-block params) and allows the center qubit to be entangled in a more expressive, trainable way with minimal disruption to the fixed data map and training loop. The rest of the architecture (RX feature map, data re-uploading, and CZ pattern) remains unchanged.
</DESCRIPTION>
