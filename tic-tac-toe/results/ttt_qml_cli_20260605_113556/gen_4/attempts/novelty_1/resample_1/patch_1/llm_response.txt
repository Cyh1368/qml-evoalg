<DESCRIPTION>
Propose two targeted enhancements to the ANSATZ_SPEC to improve expressivity and training efficiency without changing the training loop or data/feature map:
1) Duplicate the fixed CZ entangling layer within each ansatz block to increase circuit depth and expressivity, allowing the model to capture more complex correlations in tic-tac-toe boards.
2) Split the four CRX gates that connect to the center qubit into two parameter groups (crx_left and crx_right) by sharing parameters across symmetric pairs. This increases parameter efficiency while enabling richer, symmetry-informed entanglement patterns around the center.
These changes are confined to the ANSATZ_SPEC and keep adjacency constraints satisfied. They should modestly increase depth and parameter count in a controlled way, with the potential to improve validation/test performance and reduce generalization gap.
</DESCRIPTION>
