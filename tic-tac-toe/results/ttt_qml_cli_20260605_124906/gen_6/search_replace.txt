Replace the CZ perimeter gates with a single-parameter, parameterized CRZ ring on the outer edge. This introduces a shared crz_ring angle across all eight perimeter edges, increasing expressivity without adding parameters per-edge. It aligns with the idea of parameter-sharing rings to boost performance while keeping parameter count modest. This change should improve validation/test metrics by enabling coherent hub-to-hub perimeter phase relations.
