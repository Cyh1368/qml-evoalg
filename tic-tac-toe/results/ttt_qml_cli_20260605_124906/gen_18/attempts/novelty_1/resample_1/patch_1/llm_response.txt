<DESCRIPTION>
Introduce two additional fixed two-qubit CZ gates on center-to-edge connections (wires [8,1] and [8,3]) after the existing hub-entanglement CRY gates in each ansatz block. This adds non-local entanglement between the center and edges without increasing the number of trainable parameters (since CZ gates are fixed). The change stays within adjacency constraints (center-to-edge pairs are valid grid edges) and keeps the overall parameter budget constant, potentially improving validation accuracy and reducing generalization gap by enhancing center-edge correlations.
