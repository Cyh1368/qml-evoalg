<DESCRIPTION>
Add two fixed CZ entangling gates between the center qubit and the adjacent edge qubits (center-edge) after the hub CRX entanglement in each ansatz block. This introduces non-parametric, non-local center-edge correlations to potentially boost expressivity and validation performance without increasing the trainable parameter count. It respects the adjacency constraints (center with edges 1 and 3 are valid edges) and keeps the overall budget constant.
</DESCRIPTION>
