<DESCRIPTION>
Introduce a two-axis center-to-edge hub entanglement pattern to increase expressivity without exploding parameter count. Specifically, split the hub connections into two parameter-sharing axes: CRZ on edges (8,1) and (8,5) share one parameter, and CRX on edges (8,3) and (8,7) share another. Keep the ring perimeter CZ gates and add CRX ring gates on the perimeter with a shared crx_ring parameter. This design variation aims to improve validation/generalization by diversifying the hub interactions while maintaining parameter efficiency comparable to prior setups that succeeded (e.g., CRX ring + CRY hub in earlier seeds) and the overall training efficiency.

</DESCRIPTION>
