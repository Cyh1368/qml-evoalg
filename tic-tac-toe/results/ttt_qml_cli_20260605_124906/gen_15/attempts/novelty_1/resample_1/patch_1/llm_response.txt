<DESCRIPTION>
Introduce parameter-sharing on the ring entanglement by switching the outer ring CZ gates to CRZ gates (shared parameter crz_ring), and diversify the hub-entanglement axes by combining CRZ (shared parameter) on some center-to-edge links with CRX (shared parameter) on others. This reduces parameter count, maintains expressivity, and can improve generalization and training stability, potentially boosting the combined score.
