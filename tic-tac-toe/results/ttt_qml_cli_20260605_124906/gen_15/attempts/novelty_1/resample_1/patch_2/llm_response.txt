<DESCRIPTION>
Replace the fixed CZ ring with shared-parameter CRZ ring to encourage smoother ring entanglement dynamics, and diversify hub entanglement by mixing CRZ and CRX gates on center-to-edge links with shared parameters. This reduces parameter count for ring, increases expressive potential at the hub, and can improve training stability and generalization, aiming to boost the combined score.
