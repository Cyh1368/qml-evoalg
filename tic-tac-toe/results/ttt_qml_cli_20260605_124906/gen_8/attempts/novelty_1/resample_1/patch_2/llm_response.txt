<DESCRIPTION>
Add a second, independent hub-edge entanglement layer by introducing four new CRZ gates from the center (qubit 8) to each edge qubit (1,3,5,7) with unique parameter keys. This augments expressivity with non-local coupling while preserving the existing hub-edge CRZ gates. The new gates sit immediately after the original hub-edge CRZ block, enabling richer parameterization without altering data handling or training loop.
